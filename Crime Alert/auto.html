Automobile Crimes In India

Car thieves usually have set agendas for their stolen vehicles. Most are motivated by greed and their actions are centred on illegal financial gain, but sometimes the thefts can be attributed to not-so-innocent fun. In 2020, less theft cases were reported across India. This was a significant drop in the number of cases compared to the previous fiscal years. Overall, an exponential increase in the theft cases was seen in the country from 2005.
