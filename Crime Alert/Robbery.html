Robbery Cases In India

The Indian capital city of Delhi had the highest number of robberies, with over 1.9 thousand cases reported in 2020. Mumbai was the city with the second highest number of reported robberies, with 718 cases reported in that year.

In 2020, over 493 thousand robbery cases were reported across India. This was a significant drop in the number of cases compared to the previous fiscal years. Overall, an exponential increase in the theft cases was seen in the country from 2005.

In 2013, robbery rate for India was 2.8 cases per 100,000 population. Robbery rate of India increased from 1.6 cases per 100,000 population in 2004 to 2.8 cases per 100,000 population in 2013 growing at an average annual rate of 6.69%. The cases of robberies in India are seen increasing from the past years in a larger rate .
